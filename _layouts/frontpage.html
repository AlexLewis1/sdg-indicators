UK data for Sustainable Development Goal indicators

This is a development website for collecting and disseminating UK data for the Sustainable Development Goal global indicators. This is a collaboration between the ONS Sustainable Development Goals team and the ONS Data Science Campus, building on the earlier work of the US. Currently data on the site may be test data. Please send any comments and feedback to sustainabledevelopment@ons.gov.uk
